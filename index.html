University of Manchester International Admissions

University of Manchester International Admissions

Every year, students from different academic backgrounds apply for admission at University of Manchester in various UG and PG programs. The application process and guidelines are uniform for international and domestic students. However, international students need to submit a few additional admission documents.

These include:

  • Official Academic transcripts from all previously attended institutions
  • Certified English translations of transcripts (if required) must be attached.
  • Exams taken to study in UK such as IELTS, GMAT, GRE
  • IELTS & TOEFL are mandatory for international students.
  • GRE/GMAT score is required for postgraduate courses.
  • Personal essays or Statement of Purpose
  • UCAS Personal Statement is requested by UG applicants.
  • Statement of Purpose is asked from PG applicants.
  • One or two Letters of Recommendation, depending on program
  • Resume (mandatory for PG applicants)
